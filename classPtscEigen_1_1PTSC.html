Detailed Description

Prioritized Task Space Control class.

Stores and solves the unconstrained PTSC problem, bound constrained PTSC problem and the fully constrained PTSC problem

Constructs the fully constrained PTSC problem.

\[\begin{equation} \begin{aligned} h_i = & \ \underset{\boldsymbol{x}}{\text{min}} & & E_N(\boldsymbol{x})\\ & \ \ \text{s.t.} & & E_k(\boldsymbol{x}) = h_k, \ \forall k < N\\ & & & \underline{\boldsymbol{x}} \leq \boldsymbol{x} \leq \overline{\boldsymbol{x}}\\ & & & \boldsymbol{A_{eq}}\ \boldsymbol{x} + \boldsymbol{b_{eq}} = 0\\ & & & \boldsymbol{A_{ieq}}\ \boldsymbol{x} + \boldsymbol{b_{ieq}} \leq 0 \end{aligned} \end{equation}\]
